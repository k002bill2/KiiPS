# Conversation Quality Evaluation Rubric

This rubric evaluates the quality of conversational AI agent interactions.
Suitable for customer support, chat assistants, and interactive agents.

Passing Score: 0.70

---

## Criteria: Understanding
Did the agent correctly understand the user's request?
- Accurately identified the user's intent
- Captured all relevant details
- Asked clarifying questions when needed
- Avoided misinterpretation

**Scoring:**
- 1.0: Perfect understanding
- 0.7: Good understanding, minor misses
- 0.5: Partial understanding
- 0.2: Significant misunderstanding
- 0.0: Completely misunderstood

---

## Criteria: Relevance
Was the response relevant to the user's query?
- Directly addressed the question
- Stayed on topic
- Avoided unnecessary tangents
- Provided requested information

**Scoring:**
- 1.0: Highly relevant response
- 0.7: Mostly relevant
- 0.5: Somewhat relevant
- 0.2: Largely irrelevant
- 0.0: Completely off-topic

---

## Criteria: Accuracy
Was the information provided accurate?
- Factually correct statements
- No hallucinations or made-up information
- Sources cited when appropriate
- Admitted uncertainty when applicable

**Scoring:**
- 1.0: Completely accurate
- 0.7: Mostly accurate, minor errors
- 0.5: Some inaccuracies
- 0.2: Significant errors
- 0.0: Major factual errors or hallucinations

---

## Criteria: Helpfulness
Was the response helpful to the user?
- Solved the user's problem
- Provided actionable guidance
- Anticipated follow-up needs
- Added value beyond the literal question

**Scoring:**
- 1.0: Extremely helpful
- 0.7: Very helpful
- 0.5: Moderately helpful
- 0.2: Minimally helpful
- 0.0: Not helpful at all

---

## Criteria: Tone & Empathy
Did the agent maintain appropriate tone?
- Professional and respectful
- Empathetic when appropriate
- Patient with the user
- Avoided condescension

**Scoring:**
- 1.0: Perfect tone throughout
- 0.7: Good tone, minor issues
- 0.5: Acceptable tone
- 0.2: Inappropriate tone at times
- 0.0: Rude or dismissive

---

## Criteria: Conciseness
Was the response appropriately concise?
- Got to the point quickly
- Avoided unnecessary verbosity
- Included only relevant information
- Easy to scan and understand

**Scoring:**
- 1.0: Perfectly concise
- 0.7: Mostly concise
- 0.5: Somewhat verbose
- 0.2: Very verbose
- 0.0: Excessively long or rambling

---

## Criteria: Follow-up Handling
Did the agent handle follow-up questions well?
- Maintained conversation context
- Built on previous exchanges
- Didn't repeat information unnecessarily
- Smoothly handled topic changes

**Scoring:**
- 1.0: Excellent follow-up handling
- 0.7: Good context maintenance
- 0.5: Acceptable continuity
- 0.2: Lost context frequently
- 0.0: No continuity between turns

---

## Weight Distribution
- Understanding: 20%
- Relevance: 15%
- Accuracy: 20%
- Helpfulness: 20%
- Tone & Empathy: 10%
- Conciseness: 10%
- Follow-up Handling: 5%
