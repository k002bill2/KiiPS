# KiiPS Project Overview

KiiPS (Korea Investment Information Processing System) is a microservices-based enterprise platform designed for venture capital and investment management. It handles various business domains including fund management, investment tracking, accounting, and limited partner (LP) relations.

## Core Purpose
To provide a unified and scalable system for investment operations, automating complex business processes in the venture capital industry.

## Key Domains
- **Fund (FD)**: Lifecycle management of venture funds.
- **Investment (IL)**: Managing investment portfolios and deals.
- **Accounting (AC)**: Financial operations and reporting.
- **System (SY)**: User permissions, settings, and common services.
- **LP (LP)**: Managing relationships with Limited Partners.
- **E-Document (EL)**: Handling electronic documents and records.
- **AI (AI)**: AI-driven chatbot and analysis services.
